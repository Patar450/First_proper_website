About Us

Who are we?

We are a passionate team who are big fans of vintage cars. Vintage Rally racing was initiated by us in 2001 in a small village in Tokyo, Japan. This small activity quickly got popular which helped us fullfill out lifelong dream to share our passion with the rest of the world.

A company founded by our passion of cars. Our motto is to ensure that every car in different eras receives as much attention and admiration as the next generation to come.
